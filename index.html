Sudoku App Support

App Features

  • Classic Sudoku gameplay
  • Three difficulty levels
  • Auto-save functionality
  • Candidate number support

How to Play

Select a difficulty level and fill in the grid with numbers 1-9. Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain all numbers 1-9 without repetition.

Account Deletion

This app does not create or maintain user accounts. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.
